Beginner Guide

New to Dota 2? Here are some key tips:

  • Start with beginner-friendly heroes like Lich, Sniper, or Wraith King.
  • Learn the map and warding spots.
  • Practice last-hitting and denying creeps.
  • Understand hero roles: Carry, Support, Offlane, Mid.
  • Watch professional games to learn strategies.

Advanced Strategies

Want to take your skills to the next level? Try these strategies:

  • Master positioning and team fights.
  • Understand power spikes and item timings.
  • Use advanced mechanics like creep pulling and stacking.
  • Learn counter-picking heroes and adapting to drafts.
  • Improve map awareness and communication with your team.
